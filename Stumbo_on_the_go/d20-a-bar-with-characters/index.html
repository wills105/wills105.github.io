D20 : A Bar With Characters

You are here: Home » Blogs » D20 : A Bar With Characters

If you haven’t been to D20, definitely give them a visit if you’re into craft beers, nerd stuff, and board games. You can talk people’s ears off about marvel, Harry Potter, DC, or even Supernatural. Nerd it up and give a local craft beer a try, like I did. This is Brach and Bone’s Campfire Candle which is an Imperial stout (dark beer with a great chocolate note with marshmallow and Graham cracker flavors as well).
